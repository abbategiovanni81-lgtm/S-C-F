The 4 Creator / Account Types (WHO)

These define tone, approval flow, and intent.

⸻

1️⃣ Brand

(Companies, apps, SaaS, ecommerce, services)

Primary goal: Trust, consistency, conversion
Voice: Neutral, professional, on-brand
Approval: Required

Typical content
	•	Product announcements
	•	Feature updates
	•	Promotions
	•	Testimonials
	•	Campaign posts

Formats they use
	•	Image posts
	•	Short promo videos
	•	Carousels
	•	Stories
	•	Reels / Shorts

App implications
	•	Approval mandatory
	•	Brand tone presets
	•	Asset attachment (logos, UI, product shots)
	•	Campaign grouping

⸻

2️⃣ Influencer

(Face-led creators, lifestyle, finance, travel, fitness)

Primary goal: Reach, authority, monetisation
Voice: Personal, opinionated
Approval: Not required

Typical content
	•	Reels / TikToks
	•	Personal takes
	•	Trend reactions
	•	Sponsored posts
	•	Story updates

Formats they use
	•	Reels
	•	TikToks
	•	Shorts
	•	Stories
	•	Carousel highlights

App implications
	•	Hook-first captions
	•	Trend-aware prompts
	•	Fast publish flow
	•	Minimal friction

⸻

3️⃣ UGC / Social

(Creators making content for brands, often faceless)

Primary goal: Deliverables, reuse, speed
Voice: Adaptive to brand
Approval: Usually required

Typical content
	•	Product demos
	•	Testimonials
	•	Before/after
	•	Ad-style clips
	•	Raw phone footage

Formats they use
	•	Vertical video (9:16)
	•	Raw clips
	•	Image + text posts
	•	Ad-ready videos

App implications
	•	Multiple versions of same content
	•	Status tracking per asset
	•	Download-ready outputs
	•	No posting required (handoff)

⸻

4️⃣ Educator

(Coaches, experts, founders teaching a topic)

Primary goal: Authority, clarity, trust
Voice: Structured, explanatory
Approval: Not required

Typical content
	•	How-to posts
	•	Step-by-step guides
	•	Explainers
	•	Myth-busting
	•	Frameworks

Formats they use
	•	Reels / Shorts
	•	Carousels
	•	Text posts
	•	Image explainers
	•	Video lessons

App implications
	•	Long-form caption support
	•	Carousel logic
	•	Save-worthy formatting
	•	Cross-platform text rewrites

⸻

The 5 Core Content Formats (WHAT)

These are the actual objects Social Command Flow manages.

⸻

1️⃣ Reels / Shorts (Video)
	•	TikTok
	•	Instagram Reels
	•	YouTube Shorts

Needs:
	•	Script
	•	Hook
	•	Caption
	•	Hashtags
	•	Voiceover (optional)
	•	Video attachment

⸻

2️⃣ UGC Video
	•	Ad-style
	•	Testimonial
	•	Demo

Needs:
	•	Multiple versions
	•	Brand-specific captions
	•	Download/export
	•	Approval state

⸻

3️⃣ Image Post
	•	Quote
	•	Promo
	•	Announcement

Needs:
	•	Caption
	•	Hashtags
	•	Image attachment
	•	Platform sizing

⸻

4️⃣ Carousel
	•	Education
	•	Comparison
	•	Storytelling

Needs:
	•	Slide-by-slide copy
	•	Caption summary
	•	Reuse logic

⸻

5️⃣ Text-First Post
	•	X / Threads
	•	LinkedIn
	•	TikTok text posts

Needs:
	•	Platform tone rewrites
	•	Line breaks
	•	Emoji control

⸻

How This Should Exist in the App (CRITICAL)

Step 1 – User selects:

Account Type: Brand / Influencer / UGC / Educator

This sets:
	•	Default tone
	•	Approval flow
	•	Caption length
	•	CTA style

⸻

Step 2 – User selects:

Content Format: Reel / UGC Video / Image / Carousel / Text

This sets:
	•	Required fields
	•	Media expectations
	•	Workflow stages
